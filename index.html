Introduction

I am a gradute student in Electrical Engineering at National Tsing Hua University (NTHU), with a strong interdisciplinary background in computer science and biomedical sciences. I am proficient in multiple programming languages, including Python, C/C++/C#, HTML, JavaScript and MATLAB. Furthermore, I have hands-on experience with frameworks such as PyTorch/TensorFlow, Django, AWS, Unity3D, Linux and Android Studio.

My research focuses on medical AI, signal processing and LLM-based automated speech recognition, with an emphasis on real-world application through interdisciplinary collaboration. I am the first author of two papers accepted at IEEE ICASSP 2024 and 2025, addressing key challenges in respiratory and cardiac sound classification. These works leverage domain-specific knowledge and clinical patnerships to design innovative augmentation and reconstruction techniques that achieve SOTA performances.
